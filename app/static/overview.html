Data Sources

  • Small Area Mental Health Index (SAMHI), 2022
  • Index of Multiple Deprivation (IMD), 2019
  • Census 2021 (health, disability, unpaid care, economic activity)
  • Community services: OpenStreetMap + CQC registers

Methodology

SAMHI is a composite of four sub-indicators: antidepressant prescribing, GP depression prevalence, mental health hospital admissions, and DLA/PIP claims. Data is aggregated at LSOA level (Lower Layer Super Output Area) — small statistical areas of approximately 1,500 people. All figures represent area-level patterns, not individual-level outcomes.
