# Outreach Chatbot -- Demo Prompts

Prompts designed to demonstrate the policy impact of using an AI chatbot to analyse neighbourhood-level mental health data across London.

## 1. Resource Allocation

> We have funding for 3 new community mental health teams. Which boroughs should get them?

> Where are the biggest gaps between mental health need and available services?

## 2. Inequality & Equity

> Which neighbourhoods have high disability rates but poor access to support services?

> How does mental health need differ between inner and outer London?

## 3. Pre/Post-COVID Impact

> Which boroughs saw the biggest deterioration in mental health since 2019?

> Are the areas that worsened most since COVID the same ones that were already struggling?

## 4. Area Briefing

> I'm commissioning services in Hackney. Give me a briefing on what the data shows and where to focus.

> What's driving high need in Barking and Dagenham and what would you recommend?

## 5. Non-Obvious Insight

> What patterns in the data would most surprise a London Assembly member?

> Which areas look low-risk on deprivation but have surprisingly high mental health need?

## Recommended Demo Sequence (top 3)

1. **"We have funding for 3 new community mental health teams. Which boroughs should get them?"** -- Shows direct commissioning value
2. **"I'm commissioning services in Hackney. Give me a briefing."** -- Shows drill-down depth
3. **"Which areas look low-risk on deprivation but have surprisingly high mental health need?"** -- Shows the tool surfacing non-obvious insight
